Maintenance & Care
01 Clean grillz with cold water after every wear.
02 Use a soft-bristle toothbrush for stubborn buildup.
03 Never use abrasive toothpaste on gold-plated items.
04 Dry thoroughly with a microfiber cloth before storing.
05 Store in the provided EGYGRILLZ protective case only.
06 Do not expose to household cleaners, bleach, or chlorine.
07 Avoid contact with perfumes, hairspray, or colognes.
08 Do not smoke while wearing dental jewelry to prevent tarnishing.
09 Remove jewelry before consuming acidic drinks or hot beverages.
10 Keep away from high heat or direct sunlight for extended periods.
11 Brush and floss your natural teeth before inserting jewelry.
12 Do not use jewelry to open bottles or bite hard objects.
13 Check for loose stones weekly; do not wear if a stone is loose.
14 Deep clean once a month with professional jewelry-specific solution.
15 Do not share jewelry with others; fits are unique and hygiene-sensitive.
